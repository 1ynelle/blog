Post 1 - History of the Internet

October 2, 2020

After watching the video, I became interested in the internet of the early 2000s and what turned it into such a major part of our lives. I found that prior to the 2000s, the internet was largely made up of static webpages, used for blogging, and emails rather than providing the interactivity of websites today.

With the arrival of a more interactive Web 2.0, commenting and sharing content paved the way for social media. Before major platforms like YouTube, Facebook, and Twitter, there were MySpace, AOL Instant Messenger, Geocities (personal websites), and Napster (pirating music).

I found this history interesting because how the internet is used has changed so much in a small time frame. A time where you couldn't find everything and everyone online already seems so long ago.
